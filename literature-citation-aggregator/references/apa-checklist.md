# APA 7 Checklist

Use this checklist before finalizing citations and references.

## In-text Citations

- One author: `(Smith, 2020)` or `Smith (2020)`.
- Two authors: `(Smith & Lee, 2020)` or `Smith and Lee (2020)`.
- Three or more authors: `(Smith et al., 2020)` from the first citation.
- Multiple sources in one citation group: alphabetical order, separated by semicolons.
- Direct quotation: include page number. Avoid quotations unless requested.
- Citation placement: cite the specific sentence or clause being supported.

## Reference Entries

Journal article:
`Author, A. A., Author, B. B., & Author, C. C. (Year). Article title in sentence case. Journal Title in Title Case, volume(issue), page-page. https://doi.org/...`

Book:
`Author, A. A. (Year). Title in sentence case and italics. Publisher.`

Book chapter:
`Author, A. A. (Year). Chapter title in sentence case. In E. E. Editor (Ed.), Book title in sentence case and italics (pp. xx-xx). Publisher. https://doi.org/...`

## Formatting Checks

- Use sentence case for article and book/chapter titles.
- Use title case for journal names.
- Include issue number when available.
- Use article number instead of pages when the journal uses article numbers.
- Use `https://doi.org/` format for DOIs.
- Do not include database names for ordinary journal articles unless required by the user/institution.
- Ensure every in-text citation appears in the reference list and every reference list entry is cited in the text.
